What is On-Bill Repayment and How to Get Started
The On-Bill Repayment Program makes energy efficiency and electrification upgrades more accessible for homeowners, business owners, schools, and agricultural producers by connecting them to rebates, financing, education, qualified contractors, equipment, and energy audits.

To get started, please review the table below to see whether you qualify for additional programs or discounts based on your income. We want to make sure we’re offering you the lowest cost option possible!

If you do not qualify, simply complete the interest form by selecting your co-op from the provided list below. After submission, you'll receive communications primarily from the Collective Clean Energy Fund and Zero Homes—be sure to check your email, including spam and junk folders, for important updates and next steps.

Have questions? Call 720-263-2313

Your upgrades could be free

We’re glad you’re interested in the Electrify & Save On-Bill Repayment Program. However, you may qualify for free energy efficiency and electrification services based on your income.

We recommend contacting your local Weatherization Assistance Program to see if you qualify. You can easily find your provider at https://energyoffice.colorado.gov/weatherization-assistance-program.

Below is a table to help you check if you qualify based on your household size.*

Keep in mind that income thresholds are higher in some areas. If you live in a higher-income area—like Gunnison County or San Miguel County—please contact Housing Resources of Western Colorado at (970) 241-2871. For El Paso County, contact the Energy Resource Center at (719) 591-0772. These are just a few examples, so check with your local program if you think you may qualify.

*Household members are people who live with you for whom you have financial responsibilities, not roommates.
