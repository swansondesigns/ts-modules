Our Community Benefits Program

The Community Benefits Program fosters meaningful, long-term partnerships with the communities we serve by engaging early and often to understand and address local priorities. Through transparent collaboration and accountable action, Tri-State delivers benefits that enhance communities' quality of life, stimulate economic growth, and support sustainable development. By aligning our energy projects with community needs and maximizing the impact of our investments, we strengthen trust, create shared value, and ensure Tri-State remains a catalyst for positive change.
