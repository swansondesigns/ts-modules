Quality Install Program

Tri-State’s Quality Install (QI) training program provides in-depth training on the latest technology and advancements in electric heat pumps and heat pump water heaters. These systems deliver superior efficiency, comfort, and performance. Select the appropriate option below based on the type of installation you’re performing.

Contractors can receive up to $450 per HVAC installation as a Quality Install (QI) trade ally with Tri-State

The Tri-State Quality Install Program for HVAC provides a $350 to $450 contractor spiff per install. The program focuses on equipment selection and solutions for 5F and includes static pressure and air flow testing for ducted systems. Qualifying customer systems must be a member of and receive electric service from one of Tri-State G&T’s participating cooperatives or public power district. Find a list of our member systems here.

Contractors can receive up to $200 per Heat Pump Water Heater installation as a Quality Install (QI) trade ally with Tri-State.

Qualifying customer systems must be a member of and receive electric service from one of Tri-State G&T’s participating cooperatives or public power district. Find a list of our member systems here.

Apply to the QI program through one of options below

If accepted, you agree to be listed on Tristate.coop, LoveElectric.org and/or participating member websites. You also agree to annual program updates and future requirements to maintain program approval.

1

Take an approved air source heat pump course from a manufacturer or training provider and email to peter.rusin@tristategt.org

2

Verify you are approved by PRPA's Efficiency Works Program or on Xcel Colorado's Heat Pump Contractor list by sending a screenshot to peter.rusin@tristategt.org

3

Watch the 4 module training videos below and answer the questionnaire on this webpage.

4

A representative from Tri-State will contact you within 5 business days on your Quality Install Program status.
